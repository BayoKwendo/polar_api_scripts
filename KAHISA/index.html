About Us


KAHISA stands for KENYA ADOLESCENT HIV IMPELEMENTATION SCIENCE ALLIANCE

Mission: To build implementation science capacity among stakeholders working in the field of Adolescent and Young Adult HIV in Kenya

Vision:  To halt the HIV pandemic among AYA and improve the outcomes of AYA living with HIV in Kenya by building implementation science capacity among all the stakeholders
